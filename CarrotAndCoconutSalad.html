Carrot Coconut Salad

From Nelson’s Kitchen
Print

Adapted from The Complete Indian Cookbook by Mridula Baljekar

Ingredients:
 
  • 225gr/8oz carrots
  • 2 teaspoon of dissecated coconut
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • chopped parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt (or to taste)
Procedure:
 
  • Peel and grate the carrots
  • Heat up the cumin seeds in a dry skillet until fragrant.
  • Either crush the cumin seeds with a heavy saucepan or pulse them in a coﬀee grinder.
  • Mix all the ingredients in a bowl, except the salt.
  • Right before serving sprinkle the salt and mix.
